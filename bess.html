Next-Gen Energy Storage

Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS) are critical for modern grid stability and energy arbitrage. Eclipse Solar provides comprehensive testing protocols to ensure your storage assets are performing to specification and maintaining healthy capacity.


Core Testing Capabilities

Capacity Testing

Accurate measurement of usable energy storage to track degradation and state-of-health over time.

Round-Trip Efficiency

Analyzing precision energy losses during charge and discharge cycles to maximize arbitrage value.

Thermal Management

Critical verification of cooling systems to prevent overheating and mitigate lithium-ion fire risks.

Communication Protocols

Ensuring seamless integration between BESS controllers, inverters, and utility-scale grid systems.
